Crispy Korean Wings

These wings are perfect when you're craving wingstop but don't want to spend too much money. This dish is full of flavor and spices. I guarantee the whole family will love!

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Wings
  • Brown Sugar
  • Gochujang
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Rice Wine Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Ginger

Steps:

  1. Marinate
  2. Grill
  3. Make Sauce
  4. Brush & garnish
